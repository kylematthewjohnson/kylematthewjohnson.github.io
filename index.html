Kyrin Grey

dancer | choreographer | producer | director | story teller | complex human person

Kyrin Grey creates contemporary dance theatre shaped by story, structure, and sound. A former professional dancer who transitioned to a career in data science before returning to the stage, Grey's work reflects a mind equally attuned to musical architecture and emotional narrative. He draws from an eclectic musical palette that spans genres and eras and loves to use live music in his performances. He continues to perform in his own productions and in collaboration with local artists and arts organizations.

The Infinite Loop

1 hour | 2 dancers | premiered in 2024 at BASE Experimental Arts + Space

The Infinite Loop revolves around two characters. In some way these two are like mirror images reflecting back at one another. They are also strangers. Strangers seeking connection in a disconnected world. Strangers who find themselves trapped together in a strange room with a strange door.

Choreography: Kyrin Grey and Rhea Keller

Dancers: Derek Crescenti, Lara Seefeldt

Lighting Design + Technical Direction: Catriona Urquhart

Now We Are Five

1 hour | 5 dancers, 5 musicians | premiered in 2024 at Erickson Theatre

Now We Are Five explores the dynamics of the interconnected relationships of five individuals as they navigate the difficulties of challenging traditional narratives on romance and celebrate the beauty of love in all its forms.

Choreography: Kyrin Grey and the dancers

Dancers: Rodrick Barnes, Kyrin Grey, Rhea Keller, Leah Russell, kyle sangil

A Story of Love OR Death

15 minutes | 3 dancers | premiered in 2023 at Yaw Theater

A Story of Love OR Death is a classic love triangle. Inga loves Olga. Helga loves Olga. Olga loves... Olga? Created for the Wielding Forms Dance Festival, co-produced by Coriolis Dance and Yaw Theater. The festival explored the element of chance in live performance. Embracing this challenge, everything from the music and dancer footwear to the ending of the story were all decided by a coin flip, a dice roll, a card draw, and a game of rock, paper, scissors.

Choreography: Kyrin Grey

Dancers: Rhea Keller, Leah Russell, Lara Seefeldt

Lighting Design + Technical Direction: Stella Kutz

Reckoning

45 minutes | 4 dancers | premiered in 2022 at BASE Experimental Arts + Space | filmed at NOD Theater

Reckoning is a series of imagined conversations between multiple versions of Kyrin throughout his career as a dancer. Each represent Kyrin at an important crossroads in life. This highly personal work was born out of his transition from dance to data science and marked the beginning of his new adventures in choreography.

Choreography: Kyrin Grey

Dancers: Rhea Keller, Ivana Lin, Alicia Pugh, Leah Russell

Lighting Design + Technical Direction: Logan Thomas

Profile

Kyrin Grey is a dancer and choreographer whose work bridges contemporary dance, theatre, and live music performance.

Kyrin performed throughout the Pacific Northwest for over a decade with companies including Whim W'Him, Seattle Opera, Village Theatre, and City Opera Ballet. He danced in original works by choreographers including Annabelle Lopez Ochoa, Olivier Wevers, Penny Saunders, Joshua Peugh, Ihsan Rustem, Jennifer Porter, Bennyroyce Royon, Sonia Dawkins, and Sara de Luis.

Kyrin took a hiatus from performing to pursue a degree in Computer Science from the University of Washington. He currently works as a Data Scientist and Engineer while continuing his choreographic pursuits.

Since returning to dance, Kyrin has presented three independent dance productions, premiered original works at local and national dance festivals, and continues to collaborate with local artists and arts organizations.
