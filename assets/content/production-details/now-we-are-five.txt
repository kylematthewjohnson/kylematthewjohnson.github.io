TITLE:
Now We Are Five
CONCEPT:
Kyrin Grey
CHOREOGRAPHY:
Kyrin Grey and the dancers
YEAR:
2024
DURATION:
1 hour
CAST-SIZE:
5 dancers, 5 musicians
PREMIERE VENUE:
Erickson Theatre
DESCRIPTION:
Now We Are Five explores the dynamics of the interconnected relationships of five individuals as they navigate the difficulties of challenging traditional narratives on romance and celebrate the beauty of love in all its forms.
